Welcome to Troop 49!

Troop 49 is a Scouting America Troop based in Bayside, Queens. We provide young men with leadership and enrichment that allows them to grow into meaningful members of their community.

End of year

Our final meeting of the year will be held on June 13th, where we will celebrate our Spring Court of Honor! Please make all efforts to attend the final meeting of the year where we will celebrate our accomplishments throughout the year, and give important information about the summer and the upcoming 2025-2026 Scouting year!
